Why Zadar.

Zadar offers a rarer Adriatic balance of old-town texture, island access, coastal living and architectural atmosphere.

Its appeal is quieter than many headline coastal destinations, which makes it particularly relevant to buyers looking for atmosphere, discretion and long-view quality.
