Selected opportunities.

A broader view of the kinds of homes, projects and private opportunities LUX may introduce across Marche.

Not everything begins with a listing.

Selected Opportunities is designed to show the kinds of properties and projects that may become relevant through LUX.

Some may be publicly presented.

Others may remain private until introduced through a more tailored process.

This creates a more selective and flexible way for buyers to discover what the region can offer.

Character properties

Homes with architectural identity, atmosphere and strong sense of place.

Why selected opportunities matter.

The most relevant opportunity is not always the one that is easiest to publish.

In regions like Marche, some of the most compelling possibilities emerge through timing, local relationships and buyer fit.

A more selective process allows for better alignment between place, property and buyer intention.
